On October 12, 2010, the TSP issued [Bulletin 10-U-7]({{ site.baseurl }}/bulletins/10-u-7.pdf){:target="\_blank"}, _Returned Mail Report for Agencies and Uniformed Services_. This bulletin provided instructions for agencies and services to identify and submit corrected address information for TSP participants who were identified as actively employed, and for whom the TSP had received at least one materials mailing returned as undeliverable.
